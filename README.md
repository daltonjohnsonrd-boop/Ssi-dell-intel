## Time‑based lease‑to‑own clause set

You can drop this into a short agreement:

1. Lease and use  
   The Lessor leases the Equipment to the Lessee on a **time‑based** rental basis, with an option to acquire ownership after payment of a specified number of billable hours.[1][3] The Lessee may use the Equipment only for lawful business purposes and in accordance with the manufacturer’s instructions.[4]

2. Measurement of time and billing  
   - “Billable Hours” means hours of operation recorded by the Equipment’s built‑in hour meter, telematics device, or other agreed tracking system.  
   - Lessor will invoice the Lessee monthly for all Billable Hours in the prior month at the Hourly Use Rate, subject to the Minimum Billing Block.  
   - If the tracking system fails, the parties will use reasonable backup records (job logs, operator logs) or agree on a good‑faith estimate.  

3. Lease‑to‑own structure  
   - Each invoice for Billable Hours is treated as rent under this Agreement.  
   - Once the Lessee has paid for at least [X] Billable Hours in aggregate (the “Ownership Threshold”), the Lessee will have fully paid the lease‑to‑own price.  
   - Upon payment of all invoices up to the Ownership Threshold and any other amounts due, title to the Equipment automatically transfers to the Lessee “as‑is, where‑is,” with no further purchase price.[5][6]

4. Term and early termination  
   - Initial Term: The lease begins on the Lease Start Date and continues until the earlier of (a) Lessee reaching the Ownership Threshold and paying all amounts due, or (b) the Lease End Date.[6]
   - Either party may terminate early on 30 days’ written notice. If Lessee terminates before reaching the Ownership Threshold, Lessee must return the Equipment at its expense and remains liable for all unpaid invoices for Billable Hours up to the return date.  

5. Taxes and regulatory fees  
   - Lessee is responsible for all applicable sales, use, personal property, and short‑term lessor or similar rental fees or taxes imposed on the time‑based rental of the Equipment in Nevada or any other jurisdiction of use.[7][8]
   - Lessor may add such taxes and fees as separate line items on invoices.  

6. Maintenance, repairs, and loss  
   - Lessee must keep the Equipment in good working order, perform routine maintenance, and promptly notify Lessor of any malfunction.[1][4]
   - Lessee bears the risk of loss, theft, or damage while the Equipment is in Lessee’s possession and must insure it for at least its replacement value, naming Lessor as loss payee.  

7. Default and remedies  
   Lessee is in default if it (a) fails to pay any invoice within [10–15] days after the due date, (b) fails to maintain or insure the Equipment, or (c) becomes insolvent or files bankruptcy. On default, Lessor may:  
   - Suspend Lessee’s use rights and remotely disable the Equipment if technically possible.  
   - Repossess the Equipment.  
   - Accelerate all unpaid amounts then due and pursue other remedies allowed by law.[1][2]

8. Ownership status and option  
   - Until the Ownership Threshold is reached and all sums are paid, Lessor is the owner of the Equipment for all purposes, and Lessee has only a leasehold interest with an option to acquire title by fulfilling the Threshold.[9][1]
   - The parties intend this to be treated as a true lease with an option to purchase for tax and accounting purposes unless recharacterized under applicable law.[9][2]

9. Return of equipment (if no ownership)  
   If the lease ends or is terminated before the Lessee reaches the Ownership Threshold, Lessee must return the Equipment to Lessor, in good condition (ordinary wear and tear excepted), at the location designated by Lessor, at Lessee’s cost.[6][4]

10. Nevada law  
   This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Nevada, without regard to its conflicts of laws rules. The parties acknowledge that Nevada may impose specific licensing and regulatory requirements on commercial equipment lessors and agree to cooperate in complying with such requirements.[2][8]

Below is a clean, fully formatted contract using the structure we drafted, tailored for a time‑based lease‑to‑own equipment deal in Nevada.

> Non‑lawyer sample. Have a Nevada attorney review and customize before you use it in production.

***

# TIME‑BASED EQUIPMENT LEASE‑TO‑OWN AGREEMENT

This Time‑Based Equipment Lease‑to‑Own Agreement (“Agreement”) is made as of ____________, 20____ (the “Effective Date”), by and between:

**Lessor (Owner):** __________________________________________  
Address: ____________________________________________________  
Phone / Email: ______________________________________________  

**Lessee (Customer):** ________________________________________  
Address: ____________________________________________________  
Phone / Email: ______________________________________________  

Lessor and Lessee may each be referred to as a “Party” and together as the “Parties.”

***

## 1. Equipment; Schedule

1.1 **Equipment.** Lessor leases to Lessee the equipment described below (the “Equipment”):

- Description: _____________________________________________  
- Make / Model: ____________________________________________  
- Serial Number(s): ________________________________________  
- Accessories / Attachments: ________________________________  

1.2 **Schedule.** If the Parties wish to add additional equipment later, they may sign supplemental schedules referencing this Agreement, which will be incorporated by reference.

***

## 2. Term; Location

2.1 **Term.** The term of this Agreement (the “Term”) commences on _____________, 20____ (the “Lease Start Date”) and continues until the earlier of:

- (a) the date on which Lessee reaches the Ownership Threshold (defined in Section 4) and pays all amounts then due; or  
- (b) _____________, 20____ (the “Lease End Date”); or  
- (c) earlier termination under this Agreement.

2.2 **Location of Use.** Lessee shall primarily use and store the Equipment at:  
____________________________________________________________  
Lessee shall not remove the Equipment from this location without Lessor’s prior written consent, except for ordinary transportation to job sites in the normal course of Lessee’s business.

***

## 3. Time‑Based Rent and Billing

3.1 **Definitions.**

- “Billable Hours” means the hours of operation recorded by the Equipment’s built‑in hour meter, telematics device, or other mutually agreed tracking system.  
- “Hourly Use Rate” means $________ per Billable Hour.  
- “Minimum Billing Block” means ____ hours per billing cycle (if no minimum, insert “0”).

3.2 **Measurement of Time.** Billable Hours will be determined using the Equipment’s hour meter, telematics, or other agreed system. If such system fails or data is unavailable, the Parties will use reasonable backup records such as job logs or operator logs, or will agree in good faith on an estimate.

3.3 **Invoicing and Payment.**

- Lessor shall invoice Lessee monthly for all Billable Hours incurred during the prior month at the Hourly Use Rate, subject to the Minimum Billing Block.  
- Payment is due within _______ days after the invoice date (the “Due Date”).  
- Payments shall be made by: [check / ACH / wire / card] to the account designated by Lessor.  

3.4 **Late Charges.** Any payment not received by the Due Date may incur a late fee of __________% of the overdue amount or $__________, whichever is greater, plus interest at _______% per month (or the maximum allowed by law, if lower), until paid.

3.5 **Security Deposit (if any).** Lessee shall pay a security deposit of $___________ upon signing. Lessor may apply the deposit to unpaid rent, damage, or other amounts due. Any remaining balance will be returned within _______ days after the end of the Agreement, provided Lessee has fully performed its obligations.

***

## 4. Lease‑to‑Own Structure and Ownership Threshold

4.1 **Ownership Threshold.** Lessee will obtain ownership of the Equipment once it has paid for a total of __________ Billable Hours (the “Ownership Threshold”), calculated at the Hourly Use Rate, and all other amounts due under this Agreement are paid in full.

4.2 **Nature of Payments.** All payments for Billable Hours are deemed rent unless and until the Ownership Threshold is reached. If the Ownership Threshold is never reached, this Agreement remains a lease only and Lessee will not acquire ownership of the Equipment.

4.3 **Transfer of Title.** Upon:

- (a) Lessee reaching the Ownership Threshold; and  
- (b) payment in full of all invoices, fees, taxes, and other sums due under this Agreement,  

Lessor shall transfer title to the Equipment to Lessee, “AS‑IS, WHERE‑IS,” without any further purchase price, via a simple bill of sale or other reasonable transfer document. From that date, Lessee becomes the owner of the Equipment.

4.4 **Tax and Accounting Intent.** The Parties intend this transaction to be treated as a true lease with an option to acquire ownership, unless recharacterized by applicable law or by mutual agreement for tax or accounting purposes. Each Party shall consult its own advisors regarding tax treatment.

***

## 5. Taxes and Regulatory Fees

5.1 **Responsibility for Taxes.** Lessee shall be responsible for all applicable sales, use, personal property, rental, short‑term lessor, and similar taxes, fees, and governmental charges arising from the lease, use, or ownership transfer of the Equipment, excluding Lessor’s income or franchise taxes.

5.2 **Invoicing of Taxes.** Lessor may separately itemize and invoice such taxes and fees, and Lessee shall pay them as part of the monthly invoice.

***

## 6. Use, Maintenance, and Repairs

6.1 **Permitted Use.** Lessee shall use the Equipment:

- only for lawful purposes,  
- in a careful and proper manner, and  
- in accordance with the manufacturer’s instructions and all applicable laws and regulations.

6.2 **Maintenance.** Lessee shall, at its expense:

- keep the Equipment in good working order and condition,  
- perform all routine maintenance recommended by the manufacturer, and  
- promptly notify Lessor of any malfunction, defect, or damage.

6.3 **Repairs.** Lessee is responsible for all repairs (other than those expressly assumed by Lessor in writing). Lessor may require Lessee to use authorized service providers or approve major repairs in advance.

6.4 **No Alterations.** Lessee shall not alter, modify, or attach any non‑standard accessories to the Equipment without Lessor’s prior written consent, except for ordinary and reversible attachments used in the course of business.

***

## 7. Risk of Loss; Insurance

7.1 **Risk of Loss.** From delivery of the Equipment to Lessee until it is either returned to Lessor or ownership transfers to Lessee under Section 4, Lessee bears all risk of loss, theft, destruction, or damage, regardless of cause.

7.2 **Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain, at its own expense, insurance reasonably acceptable to Lessor, including:

- Property insurance covering the Equipment for at least its replacement value, naming Lessor as loss payee; and  
- Liability insurance with limits of not less than $________ per occurrence, naming Lessor as an additional insured.

7.3 **Proof of Coverage.** Upon request, Lessee shall provide certificates of insurance evidencing the required coverage.

***

## 8. Ownership Status; No Encumbrances

8.1 **Ownership Prior to Transfer.** Until title transfers under Section 4, Lessor is and shall remain the sole owner of the Equipment. Lessee has only a leasehold interest and a contractual right to acquire ownership by satisfying the Ownership Threshold and all payment obligations.

8.2 **No Liens or Transfers.** Lessee shall not sell, assign, sublease, pledge, encumber, or otherwise transfer any interest in the Equipment or this Agreement without Lessor’s prior written consent.

***

## 9. Default and Remedies

9.1 **Events of Default.** Lessee will be in default if:

- (a) Lessee fails to pay any amount when due and such failure continues for _____ days after written notice;  
- (b) Lessee materially breaches any other term of this Agreement and fails to cure within _____ days after written notice;  
- (c) Lessee fails to maintain required insurance;  
- (d) Lessee becomes insolvent, makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors, or files or has filed against it any bankruptcy or similar proceeding.

9.2 **Remedies.** Upon default, Lessor may, in addition to any other remedies available under law:

- (a) declare all amounts then outstanding immediately due and payable;  
- (b) suspend Lessee’s right to use the Equipment and, if technologically feasible, remotely disable the Equipment;  
- (c) enter any premises where the Equipment is located and repossess it, without breach of the peace;  
- (d) recover damages, costs of collection, and reasonable attorneys’ fees.

9.3 **No Waiver.** Lessor’s failure to exercise any remedy shall not be a waiver of that remedy or any other remedy.

***

## 10. Early Termination and Return

10.1 **Lessee’s Early Termination.** Lessee may terminate this Agreement before the Ownership Threshold is reached by:

- giving at least ______ days’ prior written notice; and  
- paying all amounts then due and payable through the effective date of termination; and  
- returning the Equipment as described below.

10.2 **Condition at Return.** Upon termination or expiration before ownership transfers, Lessee shall return the Equipment to Lessor at _____________________________, at Lessee’s expense, in good condition and working order, ordinary wear and tear excepted.

10.3 **Failure to Return.** If Lessee fails to return the Equipment as required, Lessor may treat such failure as a default, recover the replacement value, and pursue all remedies available at law or in equity.

***

## 11. Disclaimers; Limitation of Liability

11.1 **As‑Is.** EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY STATED IN A SEPARATE WRITTEN WARRANTY SIGNED BY LESSOR, THE EQUIPMENT IS LEASED “AS‑IS, WHERE‑IS,” AND LESSOR MAKES NO OTHER WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING ANY IMPLIED WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY OR FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE.

11.2 **Limitation of Liability.** TO THE MAXIMUM EXTENT PERMITTED BY LAW, NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR ANY SPECIAL, INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES ARISING OUT OF OR RELATING TO THIS AGREEMENT, EVEN IF ADVISED OF THE POSSIBILITY OF SUCH DAMAGES. LESSOR’S AGGREGATE LIABILITY SHALL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL AMOUNT OF RENT PAID BY LESSEE UNDER THIS AGREEMENT.

***

## 12. Notices

All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by hand, certified mail, recognized overnight courier, or email (with confirmation) to the addresses set forth above, or to such other address as a Party may designate in writing.

***

## 13. Assignment

Lessee shall not assign or transfer this Agreement or any rights or obligations hereunder without Lessor’s prior written consent. Lessor may assign this Agreement and/or sell or assign its interest in the Equipment, provided such assignment does not reduce Lessee’s rights under this Agreement.

***

## 14. Governing Law; Venue

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Nevada. Any action arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be brought in the state or federal courts located in __________ County, Nevada, and the Parties consent to the exclusive jurisdiction of such courts.

***

## 15. Miscellaneous

15.1 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to the Equipment and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous discussions, proposals, or agreements, whether oral or written.

15.2 **Amendments.** Any modification or amendment must be in a written document signed by both Parties.

15.3 **Severability.** If any provision is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

15.4 **Counterparts; Electronic Signatures.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which will be deemed an original, and all of which together constitute one instrument. Signatures delivered by electronic or digital means shall be deemed original signatures.

***

## 16. Acknowledgment

By signing below, the Parties acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms of this Agreement.

***
